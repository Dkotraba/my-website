Welcome to Drew's Game Corner

Drew's Game Corner is a small gaming lounge where we connect you with your local community through various types of game.

Our Goal

Our goal at Drew's Game Corner is provide a safe and relaxing place to grow a local community of game enthusiasts alike.

Our Services

Wether your looking for the space and know-how to host your own Dungeons and Dragons campaign or you just want a relaxing place to play Super Smash Bros with your friends we try to accommodate

  • Cards and Board Games We have a large selection of board games and spare decks for the most popular card games
  • Consoles We have many tvs and a one of pretty much every console made as well as over hundreds of games for them.(Don't be afraid to bring your own in.)
  • Computers We are stocked with 12 brand new computers with only the best hardware and peripherals for you and your friends.
  • VR Lab The Game Corner is equipped with 2 Occulus rift with updated games library(Members Only)
  • Snacks We also carry a variety of refreshing drinks and tasty snacks in our cafe section

Looking to compete? See our Events page to see our upcoming tournament schedules. Amatuers and Pros welcome!
